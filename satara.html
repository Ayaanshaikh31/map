Satara


Pincode: 415001
Satara is a picturesque district located in the western part of Maharashtra.
Known for its stunning landscapes, historical forts, and cultural heritage, Satara is a popular destination for nature lovers and history enthusiasts.
The district is also famous for its unique geographical features, including the Kaas Plateau, a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Satara:

  • Kaas Plateau: Also known as the "Valley of Flowers," it is a UNESCO World Natural Heritage site famous for its seasonal wildflowers.
  • Sajjangad Fort: A historic fort and the final resting place of Saint Ramdas, offering panoramic views and a spiritual experience.
  • Thoseghar Waterfall: A stunning waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, making it a popular spot for nature enthusiasts.
  • Ajinkyatara Fort: A historic fort offering breathtaking views of the Satara city and the surrounding landscapes.
  • Panchgani: A scenic hill station known for its beautiful viewpoints, strawberry farms, and pleasant climate.
  • Mahabaleshwar: A famous hill station offering lush green valleys, majestic peaks, and a variety of tourist attractions.
  • Vajrai Waterfall: One of the highest waterfalls in India, located in the Sahyadri mountain range.
  • Koyna Dam: A massive dam built on the Koyna River, known for its scenic beauty and hydroelectric project.
  • Pratapgad Fort: A historic fort known for its strategic importance and the famous battle between Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan.
  • Satara City: The district headquarters, known for its cultural heritage, ancient temples, and vibrant markets.
