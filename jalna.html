Jalna


Pincode: 431203
Jalna is a city located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical and cultural heritage, Jalna is home to several ancient temples, forts, and monuments.
The city is also known for its agricultural production, particularly pulses and cotton, and its contribution to the industrial sector.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Jalna:

  • Ambad: Famous for the Ambad Jain Temple, a pilgrimage site for Jains, known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.
  • Jalna Fort: An ancient fort built during the Mughal period, offering panoramic views of the city and its surroundings.
  • Muktai Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Goddess Muktai, known for its spiritual ambiance and religious festivals.
  • Shri Ganpati Temple, Shegaon: A temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, attracting devotees and tourists for its serene atmosphere and divine blessings.
  • Kharosa Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 8th century, featuring intricate carvings and sculptures.
  • Bhadrawati Palace: A historic palace known for its architectural grandeur and cultural significance, showcasing Jalna's royal heritage.
  • Shivneri Fort: A hill fort with historical significance, offering trekking opportunities and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Wagheshwar Lake: A scenic lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for picnics, boating, and enjoying nature.
  • Sonpeth: A town known for its temples, including the Veer Bhadra Temple and Gopalji Maharaj Temple, attracting pilgrims and history enthusiasts.
  • Kundalwadi: A picturesque village known for its ancient temples and serene ambiance, perfect for spiritual retreats and relaxation.
