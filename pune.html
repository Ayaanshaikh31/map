Pune


Pincode: 411001
Pune is a vibrant city located in the western part of Maharashtra.
Known as the "Oxford of the East," Pune is famous for its educational institutions, cultural heritage, and historical significance.
The city also has a thriving IT industry and is known for its pleasant climate, beautiful gardens, and bustling nightlife.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Pune:

  • Shaniwar Wada: A historic fortification in the city, known for its architectural beauty and rich history.
  • Aga Khan Palace: A majestic palace with beautiful gardens, known for its historical significance and connection to Mahatma Gandhi.
  • Dagdusheth Halwai Ganapati Temple: A famous temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, known for its beautiful idol and vibrant festivals.
  • Sinhagad Fort: A historic fort located on a hill, offering panoramic views and trekking opportunities.
  • Pataleshwar Cave Temple: An ancient rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the heart of Pune.
  • Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum: A museum showcasing an extensive collection of artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and musical instruments.
  • Parvati Hill: A scenic hilltop offering beautiful views of the city, with temples and a museum at the summit.
  • Osho Ashram: A spiritual center and meditation resort known for its serene environment and beautiful gardens.
  • Pune Okayama Friendship Garden: A Japanese-style garden offering a peaceful retreat with lush greenery and water features.
  • Bund Garden: A popular park located on the banks of the Mula-Mutha River, offering boating facilities and recreational activities.
