Ratnagiri


Pincode: 415612
Ratnagiri is a picturesque coastal district located in the Konkan region of Maharashtra.
It is known for its stunning beaches, historical forts, and lush green landscapes. Ratnagiri is famous for its Alphonso mangoes, which are highly sought after for their taste and quality.
The district is a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and historical significance, making it a popular destination for tourists.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Ratnagiri:

  • Ganpatipule Beach: A beautiful beach with clear waters and white sands, known for its serene ambiance and the Ganpatipule Temple.
  • Ratnadurg Fort: A historic fort offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and surrounding areas.
  • Thibaw Palace: A palace built for the exiled king of Burma, offering a glimpse into the colonial history of the region.
  • Pawas: A spiritual destination known for the ashram of Swami Swaroopanand, offering peace and tranquility.
  • Jaigad Fort: A sea fort located at the tip of a peninsula, offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea and the Jaigad Creek.
  • Marleshwar Temple: An ancient temple located in a cave, dedicated to Lord Shiva, surrounded by waterfalls and lush greenery.
  • Velas Beach: A serene beach known for its turtle festival, where Olive Ridley turtles come to lay their eggs.
  • Mandavi Beach: A popular beach in Ratnagiri town, known for its black sand and beautiful sunset views.
  • Ratnagiri Lighthouse: A historic lighthouse offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and Ratnagiri town.
  • Parashuram Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Parashuram, located on a hilltop offering scenic views.
