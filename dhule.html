Dhule


Pincode: 424001
Dhule is a city located in the northwest region of Maharashtra.
Known for its agricultural and industrial sectors, Dhule is an important hub for cotton and textile production.
The city is also known for its cultural heritage, with several temples, historical sites, and natural attractions.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Dhule:

  • Bhamer Fort: An ancient hill fort known for its strategic location and historical significance.
  • Torna Devi Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Goddess Torna Devi, attracting devotees and pilgrims.
  • Songir: An archaeological site with ancient Buddhist caves and sculptures, offering insights into Dhule's history.
  • Shirpur: A town known for its educational institutions, including engineering and medical colleges.
  • Nizampur: A scenic village known for its agricultural landscape and serene ambiance.
  • Indave Temple: A religious site with a historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its spiritual significance.
  • Saputara: A hill station located near Dhule, offering picturesque views, trekking trails, and a serene getaway.
  • Aner Dam: A popular spot for picnics and outdoor activities, known for its scenic beauty and tranquil surroundings.
  • Datta Mandir: A temple dedicated to Lord Dattatreya, known for its peaceful atmosphere and spiritual retreats.
  • Shivneri Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance and panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
