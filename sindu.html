Sindhudurg


Pincode: 416510
Sindhudurg is a coastal district located in the Konkan region of Maharashtra.
Known for its pristine beaches, historical forts, and lush green landscapes, Sindhudurg is a paradise for nature and history enthusiasts.
The district is named after the Sindhudurg Fort, built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, and is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Sindhudurg:

  • Sindhudurg Fort: A historic sea fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, located on an island and known for its architectural brilliance.
  • Tarkarli Beach: A pristine beach known for its clear waters, white sands, and water sports activities like snorkeling and scuba diving.
  • Malvan: A coastal town famous for its beautiful beaches, Malvani cuisine, and historic forts.
  • Vengurla Beach: A serene beach with clear blue waters, surrounded by cashew, coconut, and mango plantations.
  • Amboli: A hill station located in the Sahyadri Hills, known for its lush green landscapes, waterfalls, and pleasant climate.
  • Devgad Beach: A picturesque beach known for its tranquility, Devgad Fort, and the famous Alphonso mangoes.
  • Rock Garden: A scenic garden located in Malvan, offering beautiful views of the Arabian Sea and sunset points.
  • Shiroda Beach: A tranquil beach known for its long stretch of white sand, clear waters, and casuarina plantations.
  • Redi Ganpati Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located near the shores of the Arabian Sea.
  • Kunkeshwar Temple: A historic Shiva temple located on the shores, offering a spiritual experience and stunning sea views.
