Hingoli


Pincode: 431513
Hingoli is a district located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical significance, cultural heritage, and religious sites, Hingoli offers glimpses into Maharashtra's rich history and traditions.
The district is also known for its agricultural production, including cotton, soybeans, and pulses.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Hingoli:

  • Aundha Nagnath Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its spiritual significance and architectural beauty.
  • Hinglaj Devi Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Goddess Hinglaj, attracting devotees and pilgrims throughout the year.
  • Kalamb: A historical town with forts, temples, and ancient sites, offering insights into the region's cultural heritage.
  • Sadashivgad Fort: A hilltop fort known for its strategic location and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Parli Vaijnath Temple: A prominent pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Vaijnath (Shiva), known for its religious festivals and rituals.
  • Hingoli City: The district headquarters with bustling markets, historical landmarks, and vibrant local culture.
  • Murud: A village known for its natural beauty, agricultural fields, and peaceful ambiance, perfect for nature lovers.
  • Kelapur: A town with ancient temples, forts, and traditional Marathi architecture, offering a glimpse into the district's heritage.
  • Bhandara Dongargaon: A scenic village with picturesque landscapes, water bodies, and opportunities for rural tourism.
  • Chondi: A village known for its cultural festivals, traditional dance forms, and handicrafts, providing insights into local customs.
