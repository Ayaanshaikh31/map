Nandurbar


Pincode: 425001
Nandurbar is a district located in the northern part of Maharashtra.
Known for its tribal culture, Nandurbar is home to several indigenous tribes with unique customs and traditions.
The district is also known for its natural beauty, with lush green landscapes, rivers, and waterfalls.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Nandurbar:

  • TORANMAL: An attractive hill station of great scenic charm situated in the Satpura range.
  • MADHIKA TEMPLE: Ancient historical temple of great architectural value.
  • AKKALKUWA: Famous for the Nandurbar district's divisional headquarters.
  • NAVEGAON BANDHARA: It is situated near the border of Tapi District.
  • BHUSHAN GADH: It is situated on the Tapi banks near the village of Nandurbar.
  • DHADGAON: Famous for ancient jain caves.
  • KALAI: It is famous for riverside hill stations.
  • SHAHADA: Famous for ancient temples.
  • KAKADU: Famous for river temple.
  • SHIRPUR: Famous for ancient jain temples.
