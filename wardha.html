Wardha


Pincode: 442001
Wardha is a district located in the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical significance, Gandhian philosophy, and agricultural economy, Wardha holds a special place in India's independence movement.
The district is renowned for its educational institutions, ashrams, and cultural heritage, making it a significant destination for history and peace enthusiasts.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Wardha:

  • Sevagram Ashram: A historic ashram founded by Mahatma Gandhi, known for its association with the freedom struggle and Gandhian principles.
  • Mahatma Gandhi's Sewagram Wardha: A museum dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi, showcasing his life, philosophy, and contributions to India's independence.
  • Vinoba Bhave Ashram: An ashram dedicated to Acharya Vinoba Bhave, known for its peaceful atmosphere and contributions to the Bhoodan movement.
  • Magan Sangrahalaya: A museum focused on rural technology and traditional crafts, promoting sustainable practices and rural development.
  • Paramdham Ashram: An ashram dedicated to spiritual pursuits and Gandhian philosophy, offering retreats and workshops on sustainable living.
  • Adan River: A scenic spot for picnics and relaxation, offering serene surroundings and opportunities for boating.
  • Gandhi Hill: A hillock with panoramic views of Wardha town, named in honor of Mahatma Gandhi and his teachings.
  • Laxmi Narayan Temple: A historic temple known for its architecture and religious significance, attracting devotees from nearby areas.
  • Gitai Mandir: A temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its beautiful murals depicting scenes from the Mahabharata.
  • Santh Gajanan Maharaj Temple: A temple dedicated to Sant Gajanan Maharaj, known for its spiritual ambiance and cultural festivals.
