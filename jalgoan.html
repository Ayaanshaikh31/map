Jalgaon


Pincode: 425001
Jalgaon is a city located in the northern part of Maharashtra.
Known for its agricultural production, particularly bananas and cotton, Jalgaon is often referred to as the "Banana City" of India.
The city is also known for its educational institutions, cultural heritage, and vibrant markets.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Jalgaon:

  • Ajanta Caves: A UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves and exquisite sculptures.
  • Gandhi Teerth: A museum and research center dedicated to Mahatma Gandhi, showcasing his life and philosophy.
  • Padmalaya: A Jain pilgrimage center with a temple dedicated to Parshvanatha, attracting devotees and tourists.
  • Pal: A historic town known for its scenic beauty, ancient temples, and tranquil atmosphere.
  • Patnadevi: A revered temple dedicated to Goddess Patnai, known for its spiritual significance and festivals.
  • Muktai Nagar: An ancient town famous for its temples and historical sites, including the Muktainagar Fort.
  • Swinging Towers of Farkande: Ancient architectural marvels with swinging minarets, unique to the region.
  • Bodwad: A town known for its historic monuments, including forts and temples, offering glimpses into the past.
  • Unapdev Hot Water Springs: Natural hot water springs surrounded by scenic hills, ideal for relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Bhadgaon: A town with historical significance, known for its forts, temples, and ancient structures.
