Latur


Pincode: 413512
Latur is a city located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical forts, temples, and cultural heritage, Latur offers a glimpse into the rich history of Maharashtra.
The city is also renowned for its agricultural production, particularly for its production of pulses and oilseeds.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Latur:

  • Udgir Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance, known for its architectural marvels and panoramic views.
  • Ganjgolai: The central marketplace and cultural hub of Latur, bustling with shops, eateries, and local landmarks.
  • Ashtavinayak Temple, Latur: A temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, known for its religious importance and serene ambiance.
  • Kharosa Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 6th century, featuring intricate carvings and sculptures.
  • Siddheshwar Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, attracting devotees and pilgrims throughout the year.
  • Ausa Fort: A historic fort known for its architectural grandeur and strategic location, offering panoramic views.
  • Wadwal Nagnath Bet: A natural rock formation with ancient temples, offering a tranquil environment and scenic beauty.
  • Nilanga: A town known for its historical sites, temples, and traditional Marathi culture, perfect for cultural exploration.
  • Ambajogai: A pilgrimage site with temples dedicated to Goddess Yogeshwari and Lord Shiva, known for its religious significance.
  • Chakur: A scenic village known for its agricultural landscapes, birdwatching opportunities, and peaceful ambiance.
