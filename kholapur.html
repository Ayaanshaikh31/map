Kolhapur


Pincode: 416003
Kolhapur is a historic city located in the southwest part of Maharashtra.
Known for its rich cultural heritage, Kolhapur is famous for its temples, palaces, and vibrant traditions.
The city is also renowned for its spicy cuisine, particularly the Kolhapuri chappal, a popular handcrafted leather slipper.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Kolhapur:

  • Mahalakshmi Temple: A significant Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, known for its architectural beauty and spiritual importance.
  • Panhala Fort: A historic fort offering panoramic views of the surrounding region and a glimpse into the Maratha Empire's history.
  • Rankala Lake: A picturesque lake offering boating facilities, surrounded by gardens and recreational areas.
  • New Palace: A grand palace built in the Indo-Saracenic style, housing a museum with artifacts related to the Maratha Empire.
  • Jyotiba Temple: A revered hilltop temple dedicated to Lord Jyotiba, offering stunning views and a spiritual experience.
  • Bahubali Hill Temple: A historic Jain temple located on a hill, known for its large statue of Lord Bahubali and panoramic views.
  • Chhatrapati Shahu Museum: A museum located in the New Palace, showcasing a rich collection of artifacts, paintings, and weapons.
  • Narsinhwadi Datta Mandir: A spiritual destination located on the banks of the Krishna River, known for its serene environment.
  • Dajipur Wildlife Sanctuary: A protected area known for its rich biodiversity, including the Indian bison, deer, and various bird species.
  • Radhanagari Dam: A scenic spot ideal for picnics and nature walks, offering beautiful views of the reservoir and surrounding forests.
