Washim


Pincode: 444505
Washim is a district located in the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical heritage, cultural diversity, and agricultural economy, Washim offers a blend of traditional and modern attractions.
The district is known for its production of cotton, soybeans, and pulses, contributing significantly to Maharashtra's agricultural output.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Washim:

  • Mahadev Temple, Mahur: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its religious significance and architectural beauty.
  • Muktagiri: A pilgrimage site for Jains, known for its temples, caves, and scenic surroundings.
  • Pusad: A historical town with forts, temples, and traditional Marathi architecture, offering insights into the region's cultural heritage.
  • Balaji Temple, Washim: A popular temple dedicated to Lord Balaji (Venkateswara), attracting devotees from nearby areas.
  • Gondumari: A village known for its natural beauty, agricultural fields, and serene environment.
  • Bhandardara: A hill station near Washim known for its waterfalls, lakes, and trekking opportunities.
  • Manora Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Aundha Nagnath Temple: A prominent pilgrimage site for Hindus, known for its spiritual ambiance and religious festivals.
  • Malegaon Yatra: A religious fair held annually, attracting pilgrims and showcasing local culture and traditions.
  • Adgaon: A village known for its agricultural practices, handicrafts, and rural tourism experiences.
