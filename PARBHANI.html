Parbhani


Pincode: 431401
Parbhani is a city located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical significance, cultural heritage, and agricultural economy, Parbhani offers insights into Maharashtra's rich history and traditions.
The district is also known for its production of cotton, soybeans, and other agricultural commodities.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Parbhani:

  • Pathri: A town known for its association with Sai Baba of Shirdi and a famous dargah dedicated to him.
  • Balaghat Wildlife Sanctuary: A sanctuary known for its diverse flora and fauna, offering opportunities for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.
  • Khandoba Temple, Parbhani: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Khandoba, known for its religious festivals and cultural significance.
  • Gangakhed: A historical town with temples, forts, and ancient sites, offering glimpses into the region's architectural heritage.
  • Pimpalgaon Joge Dam: A scenic dam with serene surroundings, ideal for picnics and enjoying the natural beauty.
  • Shahnoor: A town known for its historical buildings, markets, and traditional Marathi culture.
  • Manvat: A pilgrimage site with temples and historical landmarks, attracting devotees and history enthusiasts.
  • Majalgaon Dam: A large reservoir offering picturesque views and recreational activities like boating and fishing.
  • Pingali: A village known for its agricultural landscapes and traditional lifestyle, perfect for experiencing rural Maharashtra.
  • Siddeshwar Temple, Sailu: A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architectural beauty and spiritual significance.
