Buldhana


Pincode: 443001
Buldhana is a district located in the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical significance, cultural heritage, and agricultural economy, Buldhana offers a mix of traditional and modern attractions.
The district is known for its production of cotton, soybeans, and oranges, contributing to Maharashtra's agricultural prosperity.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Buldhana:

  • Lonar Lake: A unique crater lake formed by a meteorite impact, known for its saline water and geological significance.
  • Balaji Temple, Deulgaon Raja: A prominent temple dedicated to Lord Balaji (Venkateswara), attracting devotees from all over.
  • Shegaon: Known for the Anand Sagar Lake and the Shri Gajanan Maharaj Temple, a major pilgrimage site for Hindus.
  • Nalgao Bhawani Temple: A temple dedicated to Goddess Nalgao Bhawani, known for its religious festivals and cultural significance.
  • Malkapur: A town known for its historical sites, markets, and traditional Marathi culture.
  • Morshi: A scenic town with natural beauty, agricultural fields, and peaceful surroundings.
  • Jaikwadi Dam: A large reservoir providing irrigation water and a picturesque spot for boating and picnicking.
  • Mehkar: A historical town with forts, temples, and ancient sites, offering insights into the region's rich heritage.
  • Khamgaon: Known for its markets, handicrafts, and cultural events, providing a vibrant experience of rural Maharashtra.
  • Renuka Devi Temple, Sindkhed Raja: A temple dedicated to Goddess Renuka Devi, known for its religious importance and festivals.
