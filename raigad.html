Raigad


Pincode: 402101
Raigad is a coastal district located in the Konkan region of Maharashtra.
It is known for its historical significance, scenic landscapes, and beautiful coastline. The district is home to the famous Raigad Fort, which was the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
Raigad offers a blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, making it a popular destination for tourists.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Raigad:

  • Raigad Fort: A historic fort and the former capital of the Maratha Empire, offering panoramic views and a glimpse into India's rich history.
  • Alibaug Beach: A popular beach destination known for its clean sands, water sports, and serene ambiance.
  • Kashid Beach: A beautiful beach with white sand and clear waters, ideal for relaxation and water activities.
  • Murud-Janjira Fort: A majestic sea fort located on an island off the coast, known for its invincible structure and historical significance.
  • Korlai Fort: A Portuguese fort offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea and surrounding areas.
  • Harihareshwar: A coastal town known for its ancient Shiva temple and scenic beach.
  • Revdanda Beach: A tranquil beach destination with ruins of an old Portuguese fort and a serene environment.
  • Kolaba Fort: A historic fort located in the sea, accessible during low tide, known for its architecture and historical importance.
  • Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary: A protected area known for its rich biodiversity, including various species of flora and fauna.
  • Birla Ganesh Temple: A beautifully crafted temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, offering a peaceful and spiritual experience.
