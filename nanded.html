Nanded


Pincode: 431601
Nanded is a city located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical significance, cultural heritage, and religious sites, Nanded is a major pilgrimage center for Sikhism.
The city is also known for its agricultural production, including crops like cotton, soybeans, and turmeric.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Nanded:

  • Hazur Sahib Gurudwara: One of the holiest Sikh shrines, visited by thousands of devotees every year.
  • Kandhar Fort: A historic fort with architectural remnants, offering panoramic views and insights into the region's history.
  • Isapur Dam: A scenic dam site surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for picnics and nature walks.
  • Gurudwara Nagina Ghat Sahib: A serene gurudwara located on the banks of the Godavari River, known for its peaceful ambiance.
  • Mahur: A pilgrimage site with temples dedicated to Goddess Renuka Devi and Lord Dattatreya, attracting religious devotees.
  • Basara: Known for the Saraswati Temple, a prominent pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, revered for education and wisdom.
  • Nanded Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance, offering views of the city and its surroundings.
  • Shri Guru Gobind Singh Ji Museum: A museum dedicated to the life and teachings of Guru Gobind Singh Ji, showcasing artifacts and historical documents.
  • Siddheshwar Temple: A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architecture and religious festivals.
  • Mahur Fort: A hilltop fort offering panoramic views and insights into the region's military history.
