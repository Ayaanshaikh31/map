Beed


Pincode: 431122
Beed is a city located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical significance and cultural heritage, Beed has several ancient temples, forts, and monuments.
The city is also known for its agricultural production, particularly sugarcane and cotton, and its vibrant traditional arts and crafts.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Beed:

  • Kankaleshwar Temple: A historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architectural beauty and religious festivals.
  • Parli Vaijnath Temple: One of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva, attracting devotees from across the country.
  • Maharashtra Sahitya Parishad Library: A cultural hub with a vast collection of books and manuscripts, promoting Marathi literature and culture.
  • Balgangadhar Tilak Memorial: A memorial dedicated to Lokmanya Tilak, a prominent freedom fighter and social reformer.
  • Ashtavinayak Temple: A group of eight ancient temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha, each with its unique mythology and significance.
  • Ellora Caves: A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring rock-cut caves and temples, including Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain monuments.
  • Bhandardara: A hill station and tourist destination known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and opportunities for trekking and camping.
  • Satgudi Fort: An ancient hill fort with historical significance, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Mankeshwar Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its serene ambiance and spiritual importance.
  • Chondi: A picturesque village known for its natural beauty, agricultural fields, and traditional Maharashtrian culture.
