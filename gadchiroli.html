Gadchiroli


Pincode: 442605
Gadchiroli is a district located in eastern Maharashtra, known for its tribal culture and natural beauty.
The district is rich in biodiversity with dense forests, rivers, and wildlife sanctuaries.
Gadchiroli is also known for its historical sites, festivals, and unique tribal traditions, offering a glimpse into traditional rural life.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Gadchiroli:

  • Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve: One of India's premier tiger reserves, known for its tiger population and diverse wildlife.
  • Chaprala Wildlife Sanctuary: A sanctuary known for its dense forests, home to wildlife species like sloth bears and leopards.
  • Navegaon National Park: A wildlife sanctuary offering safari rides and birdwatching opportunities amidst lush greenery.
  • Perikota Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance and panoramic views of Gadchiroli district.
  • Surjagarh Fort: A hilltop fort known for its strategic location and historical artifacts.
  • Koti Brahmanda Temple: A sacred temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architectural beauty and religious festivals.
  • Sironcha: A town situated on the banks of the Pranhita River, offering scenic views and cultural insights.
  • Prashant Dham: A serene ashram and meditation center, offering spiritual retreats and yoga sessions.
  • Markanda Mahadev Temple: A historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, attracting devotees and pilgrims.
  • Lake Chamorshi: A serene lake surrounded by forests, ideal for boating and picnics.
