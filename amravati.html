Amravati


Pincode: 444601
Amravati is a district located in the Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.
Known for its historical heritage, cultural diversity, and agricultural economy, Amravati offers a mix of traditional and modern attractions.
The district is known for its cotton production and is also an educational and industrial hub in Maharashtra.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Amravati:

  • Ambadevi Temple: A historic temple dedicated to Goddess Amba, known for its architecture and religious significance.
  • Chikhaldara Hill Station: A popular hill station known for its scenic beauty, wildlife sanctuary, and viewpoints.
  • Melghat Tiger Reserve: A wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity, including tigers, leopards, and rare species of flora and fauna.
  • Satidham Temple: A prominent temple dedicated to Lord Satyanarayan, attracting devotees from nearby areas.
  • Wadali Talao: A serene lake offering boating facilities and a peaceful environment for picnics.
  • Bhim-Kund: A historic site associated with the legend of Bhima from the Mahabharata, known for its natural rock formations and waterfall.
  • Gawilgadh Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Shri Ekvira Devi Temple: A temple dedicated to Goddess Ekvira, known for its spiritual ambiance and religious festivals.
  • Upper Wardha Dam: A picturesque dam offering scenic views, boating, and birdwatching opportunities.
  • Shri Riddhi Siddhi Ganesh Temple: A temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh, known for its cultural significance and festivals.
