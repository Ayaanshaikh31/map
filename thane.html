Thane


Pincode: 400601
Thane, often referred to as the "City of Lakes," is a major city located near Mumbai.
It is known for its scenic beauty, with numerous lakes and green spaces. Thane has a growing IT and commercial sector, contributing significantly to Maharashtra's economy.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Thane:

  • Upvan Lake: A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for boating and leisure walks.
  • Tikuji-Ni-Wadi: A popular amusement park offering a variety of rides, water park attractions, and adventure activities.
  • Yazoo Park: A family-friendly amusement park featuring rides, games, and entertainment options for all ages.
  • Tansa Lake: A picturesque reservoir known for its scenic beauty and peaceful ambiance, perfect for picnics and birdwatching.
  • Kelwa Beach: A serene beach destination offering tranquil shores, water sports, and stunning sunset views.
  • Ghodbunder Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance, offering panoramic views of Thane and its surroundings.
  • Naneghat Hills: A trekking destination with ancient caves and scenic vistas, ideal for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers.
  • Talao Pali: A centrally located lake surrounded by gardens, jogging tracks, and recreational facilities, popular among locals for evening walks.
  • Masunda Lake (Talao Pali): A bustling lake with boating facilities, surrounded by shops, eateries, and vibrant local markets.
  • Shivaji Maharaj Park: A well-maintained park dedicated to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, featuring statues, landscaped gardens, and a serene atmosphere.
