Sangli


Pincode: 416416
Sangli is a prominent city located in the southern part of Maharashtra.
Known as the "Turmeric City of Maharashtra," Sangli is famous for its agricultural produce, particularly turmeric and grapes.
The city is also known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful temples, and vibrant markets.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Sangli:

  • Ganapati Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, known for its beautiful architecture and religious significance.
  • Sagareshwar Wildlife Sanctuary: A protected area offering a variety of flora and fauna, along with scenic landscapes and trekking opportunities.
  • Krishna Valley Wine Park: A popular destination for wine enthusiasts, offering wine tours, tastings, and vineyard visits.
  • Kupwad: An industrial town known for its textile industries and manufacturing units.
  • Audumbar: A spiritual destination known for the shrine of Shri Narasimha Saraswati Swami Maharaj, attracting devotees from all over.
  • Warna Dam: A large dam built on the Warna River, providing a scenic spot for picnics and nature walks.
  • Dandoba Hill Station: A picturesque hill station offering trekking opportunities and beautiful views of the surrounding region.
  • Brahmaling Temple: An ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in a serene and peaceful environment.
  • Gokak Falls: A stunning waterfall located near Sangli, known for its scenic beauty and natural surroundings.
  • Sangli Fort: A historic fort offering a glimpse into the region's rich history and architectural heritage.
