Ahmednagar


Pincode: 414001
Ahmednagar is a historical city located in the western part of Maharashtra.
Known for its rich cultural heritage, Ahmednagar has several historical monuments, forts, and temples.
The city is also famous for its sugar and dairy industries, contributing significantly to the region's economy.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Ahmednagar:

  • Shani Shingnapur: A famous village known for its temple dedicated to Lord Shani, where houses have no doors or locks.
  • Ahmednagar Fort: A historic fort built by Ahmed Nizam Shah, offering a glimpse into the region's medieval history.
  • Renuka Devi Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Goddess Renuka, known for its religious significance and architectural beauty.
  • Meherabad: A spiritual retreat and burial place of Meher Baba, attracting followers and spiritual seekers from around the world.
  • Siddhatek: A pilgrimage site known for the Siddhivinayak Temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, situated on a hilltop.
  • Bhandardara: A hill station and lakeside destination known for its scenic beauty, waterfalls, and adventure activities.
  • Harishchandragad Fort: An ancient fort located in the Western Ghats, offering trekking opportunities and panoramic views.
  • Baglan: A historical town known for its ancient temples and archaeological sites dating back to the Satavahana period.
  • Salabat Khan's Tomb: A monument built in memory of Salabat Khan II, known for its architectural elegance and historical importance.
  • Ahmednagar Museum: A museum showcasing artifacts, sculptures, and historical exhibits related to Ahmednagar's heritage.
