Bhandara


Pincode: 441904
Bhandara is a district located in the eastern part of Maharashtra.
Known for its natural beauty, religious sites, and historical significance, Bhandara is a serene destination amidst nature.
The district is known for its lakes, temples, and cultural heritage, making it an ideal place for spiritual retreats and outdoor activities.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Bhandara:

  • Lake Wainganga: A picturesque lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for boating and picnics.
  • Nagzira Wildlife Sanctuary: A wildlife sanctuary known for its rich biodiversity, including tigers, leopards, and bird species.
  • Devi Temple, Paoni: A historic temple dedicated to Goddess Devi, attracting devotees from nearby areas.
  • Lake Lohara: A tranquil lake offering scenic views and opportunities for birdwatching.
  • Satpura Botanical Garden: A botanical garden showcasing a variety of flora native to the region.
  • Nakadongar Hill: A hilltop offering panoramic views of Bhandara district and its surrounding landscapes.
  • Markandeya Temple, Pauni: An ancient temple known for its architectural beauty and religious festivals.
  • Koka Wildlife Sanctuary: A sanctuary known for its diverse wildlife and natural habitats.
  • Chaprala Wildlife Sanctuary: A sanctuary with dense forests and wildlife species like sloth bears and wild boars.
  • Gosekhurd Dam: A scenic spot for picnics and water activities, offering serene surroundings.
