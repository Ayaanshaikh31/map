Chandrapur


Pincode: 442401
Chandrapur is a district located in the eastern part of Maharashtra.
Known for its rich mineral resources, wildlife sanctuaries, and cultural heritage, Chandrapur is often called the "Black Gold City" due to its coal mining industry.
The district is renowned for its tiger reserves, ancient temples, and natural beauty, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.

List of Popular Tourist Places in Chandrapur:

  • Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve: One of India's premier tiger reserves, known for its diverse wildlife and tiger sightings.
  • Ancient Shiva Temple at Ballarpur: A historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.
  • Gond Raja Fort: An ancient fort with historical significance, offering panoramic views of Chandrapur city and its surroundings.
  • Chandrapur Super Thermal Power Station: A prominent thermal power plant known for its engineering marvel and contribution to electricity generation.
  • Nawegaon National Park: A wildlife sanctuary known for its diverse flora and fauna, offering safari rides and birdwatching opportunities.
  • Junona Lake: A scenic lake surrounded by lush greenery, offering boating facilities and a peaceful environment.
  • Bhadravati Ganesh Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh, attracting devotees and pilgrims from nearby areas.
  • Gandhi Sagar Lake: A serene lake with picturesque surroundings, ideal for picnics and leisure activities.
  • Chandrapur Fort: A historic fort known for its architectural ruins and historical artifacts, offering insights into Chandrapur's past.
  • Mahakali Temple: A temple dedicated to Goddess Mahakali, known for its religious festivals and cultural celebrations.
